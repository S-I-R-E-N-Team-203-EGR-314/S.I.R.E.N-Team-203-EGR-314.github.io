Team Charter

Charter

  1. Each team member designs a functional Subsystem that completes their specific goal.
  2. Creating a functional weather alert device that can:
    • Measure temperature.
    • Measure light levels.
    • Alert the user a dangerous fire is happening.
    • Connect to the internet to upload collected data and change settings.
  3. Have a functioning prototype by April 26th.

Mission Statement

Our team is tasked with providing a device that uses digital technology to provide users with valuable sensory information in an emergency simulation.
