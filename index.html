Siren Team 203 - Fire Siren System Project

Team Charter

Our mission statement emerged from a brainstorming session focused on the problem we were tasked with: designing a portable weather station that uses an actuator to respond to environmental data. Given our experience with 3D printing and our understanding of motors, we realized that we could create a device specifically designed for emergency warning systems. This led us to develop a mechanism similar to a rapid siren, capable of alerting users to emergencies with efficiency and reliability.

Our mission is to provide a device that uses digital technology to provide users with valuable sensory information in an emergency simulation. Our charter includes:

  1. Designing functional subsystems with specific goals.
  2. Creating a weather alert device capable of measuring temperature, light levels, detecting fires, and connecting to the internet.
  3. Completing a functioning prototype by April 26th.
