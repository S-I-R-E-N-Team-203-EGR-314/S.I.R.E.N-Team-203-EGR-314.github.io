Fire S.I.R.E.N

Our chosen design combines durability with a compact form factor. It's constructed with a tough outer shell, making it resistant to damage while maintaining a lightweight and portable profile. This design works as a campfire alarm: when the surrounding environment becomes excessively hot and bright, a siren is triggered. This alerts users to the presence of a fire, giving them time to take safety precautions or extinguish the fire before it escalates. This would satisfy user needs as well as meet the requirements for our software requirements because of the light sensor that will detect the brightness of the fire. Then a temperature sensor to detect the heat of the fire and a motor to move a siren alarm to notify the user.
