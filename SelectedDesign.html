Fire S.I.R.E.N

We chose this design for its rugged and strong outer shell, yet it's also small, compact, and light. It is essentially a campfire alarm. When it gets too hot or bright, the siren will go off, alerting the user to a potential fire, enabling them to get to safety or put out the fire before it becomes a problem.
