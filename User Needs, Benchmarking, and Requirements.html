User Needs

List of User Needs

  • Portability and Lightweight: Device should be easy to carry.
  • Durability and Weather Resistance: It must withstand outdoor conditions.
  • Ease of Use: Setup and operation should be simple.
  • Low Maintenance: Minimal maintenance, with long-lasting batteries or solar power.
  • Loud and Clear Alarm: Audible in outdoor settings.
  • Sensitivity and Accuracy: Accurate detection with minimal false alarms.
  • Connectivity Options: Potential for smartphone or authority connectivity.
  • Affordability: Cost-effective without compromising safety.
  • Environmental Impact: Environmentally friendly, avoiding harm to nature.
  • Educational Material: Including fire safety and prevention instructions for camping.
