Hardware Implementation

This section covers the detailed hardware implementation of our project, including schematics and design rule checks (DRC).

This hardware meets the project's following requires:

Sensors(I2C): Light sensor and temp sensor.

Micro Controller: Is a 8 bit controller and works with our other componets.

Actuator: This motor driver can both control our motor and be controlled by our microcontroller via SPI.

Design Rule Check (DRC)

The Design Rule Check (DRC) is a crucial step in the PCB design process where the design is checked for any violations of design rules and standards. This ensures the manufacturability and functionality of the board.
