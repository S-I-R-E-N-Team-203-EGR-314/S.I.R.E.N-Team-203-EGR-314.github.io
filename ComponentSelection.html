Component Selection for S.I.R.E.N. Project

The document shown below is a list of components that were used to power the product and complete tasks that we wanted it to perform.

Wind Speed Sensor

Choice: Option 1 (due to budget, simplicity, and smaller form factor)

Pros Cons
Within budget (under $60), simple datasheet, analog output, ships faster, smaller form factor. Not a digital sensor, limited output wires.

Motor Driver

Choice: DRV8213DSGR (due to the motor driver's capacity and programmable capabilities)

Pros Cons
Max voltage of 11V, back and forward programmability, detailed datasheet. Unknown pins (GAINSEL, IPROPI), complex charts.

Motor

Choice: Option 2 (due to the motor's smaller size and compatibility with the design)

Pros Cons
Small, detailed datasheet, easy to install. Shaft may be too small to work with, lacks detailed information in datasheet.

Wifi Module

Choice: Mandatory (ESP 32 due to company's requirement)

Pros Cons
Bluetooth and wifi, JTAG and USART pin interfaces, built-in antenna, multi-functionality, secure boot, large memory. Some pins have usage restrictions, uses complex nomenclature, only one RTC timer, most expensive part.

Light Sensor

Choice: LTR-329ALS-01 (due to its accurate light sensing capabilities and energy efficiency)

Pros Cons
Low power consumption, broad range of light detection, power-efficient, compact size. Limited resolution, potential electromagnetic interference, susceptible to cross-talk.
