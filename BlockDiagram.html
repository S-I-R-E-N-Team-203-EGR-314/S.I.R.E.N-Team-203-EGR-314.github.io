Block Diagram

For the team's product, the design consisted of four subsystems: Temperature Sensor, Light Sensor, Siren, and the Microcontroller with Wi-Fi.

Part Selection

Wind Speed Sensor

  • Simple datasheet
  • Within budget (under $60)
  • Analog output with simple conversion
  • Ships faster and has a smaller form factor

Siren Motor Block

  • Motor Driver can handle a max voltage of 11 V
  • Possible back and forward programmability
  • Data sheet gives details on each pin and its functions

Motor

  • Small device for easy installation
  • The shaft of the motor might cause issues due to size

WiFi Module

  • Bluetooth and WiFi with JTAG and USART interfaces
  • Built-in antenna with multifunctional IO pins

Light Sensor

  • Small, compact, and power-efficient at a low cost
  • Potential electromagnetic interference and cross-talk susceptibility
